# OEM Maintenance & Operations Manual
**Equipment Name:** Heavy-Duty Centrifugal Cooling Pump
**Model Number:** Pump-101
**Revision:** 4
**Date:** 2024-02-15

## 1. Specifications
- **Operating Pressure:** 120 PSI (Standard)
- **Max Pressure:** 150 PSI
- **Flow Rate:** 500 GPM
- **Motor Coupling:** Directly coupled to Motor-55

## 2. Operating Procedures
1. Ensure Valve-204 (Inlet) is fully OPEN before startup.
2. Verify Motor-55 power supply is stable.
3. **WARNING:** Never operate Pump-101 if the operating pressure drops below 90 PSI or exceeds 140 PSI. Continuous operation at high vibration will cause catastrophic seal failure.

## 3. Maintenance Schedule
- **Monthly:** Inspect coupling alignment between Pump-101 and Motor-55.
- **Quarterly:** Lubricate motor bearings.
- **Annually:** Replace primary mechanical seal.

## 4. Troubleshooting
| Symptom | Probable Cause | Corrective Action |
|---------|----------------|-------------------|
| High Vibration | Motor-55 bearing wear or misalignment | Inspect bearings and realign coupling |
| Pressure Drop | Seal Leak | Inspect and replace mechanical seal |
